Current Effects on the World

SaveOurOceans is a website that calls for action towards pollution and littering in oceans caused by a regular person during their daily tasks. This problem is causing the planet’s lifespan to decrease. This is because the ocean is the planet’s temperature control, wildlife home, and so much more that contributes to our overall living conditions and the planet’s system.

Our reasoning is that when you think of pollution in the ocean it is from industrial causes and larger companies although a large percentage of pollution rates are from people’s daily activities.

Daily Causes

  • Marine Life Threat: Toxic chemicals, plastics, and waste harm marine species, leading to decreased biodiversity.

  • Human Health Risks: Contaminants enter the food chain through seafood, exposing humans to harmful chemicals.

  • Ecosystem Disruption: Coral reefs and habitats are damaged, reducing the ocean’s capacity to support marine life.

  • Economic Losses: Fishing, tourism, and other industries suffer as pollution harms coastal areas and marine resources.

  • Climate Change Impact: Polluted oceans absorb less CO₂, worsening climate change due to reduced carbon sequestration.
