Why is this a problem?

Pollution in the ocean is harmful because it damages marine life, destroys ecosystems, and makes the water unsafe for future generations. As plastic, chemicals, and waste build up, fish and other sea creatures die or get sick, which can lead to food shortages. The ocean also helps regulate the climate, so pollution can worsen climate change, making the planet hotter and weather more extreme. Future generations will have to deal with the long-term impacts on food, health, and the environment.

Why a website?

Creating a website about saving the ocean is important because it helps raise awareness about protecting our planet for future generations. The ocean provides food, oxygen, and a home for countless species, but pollution, overfishing, and climate change are putting it at risk. By educating others on how they can help, we can inspire people to take action, like reducing plastic waste and supporting conservation efforts. A website can reach people all over the world, making it a powerful tool to spread the message and encourage everyone to play their part in keeping the ocean healthy for years to come.
